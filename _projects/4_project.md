Designed a multimodal disentangled β-VAE architecture with audio (STFT spectrograms) and BERT text embeddings on the ICASSP 2025 Alzheimer's detection dataset for 3-class classification of healthy, MCI, and dementia speech.

**Highlights**

- Implemented fragmentation, augmentation, and fusion pipelines for multimodal learning.
- Used triplet and total correlation losses to obtain linearly separable latent clusters.
- Outperformed baselines in validation accuracy and interpretability.
- Manuscript under review at the International Journal of Pattern Recognition and Artificial Intelligence.

**Date:** May 2025
